Vision

Strategic Pillars

1. Alternative Material Innovation (AMI)

  • Challenging the assumption that technology needs to be "technological"
  • Exploring unconventional materials through a lens of unwarranted optimism

2. Ecosystem Integration

  • Seamlessly combining impractical solutions with existing infrastructure
  • Making the absurd seem mundane through strategic placement

3. Thought Leadership

  • Publishing incomprehensible white papers
  • Creating new buzzwords faster than the industry can adopt them

4. Stakeholder Confusion

  • Maintaining a level of complexity that makes questioning our methods seem unsophisticated
  • Leveraging ambiguity as a core competitive advantage
